Minor Royals

The Band

South London 5-piece Minor Royals found their groove between Talking Heads and Radiohead, before widening it to take in the indie sensibilities of The Future heads and the occasional visceral snarl of Motörhead.

Vocal & Keyboard: Silverley Allen

Guitar: David Paul Smith

Guitar: Simon Guild

Bass & Backing Vocals: Callum Ballard

Drums: Erkut Evirgen
